JCIRN

JCIRN or the Jefferson Collaborative Inquiry and Research Network is an online platform that will allow students to extend their own research opportunities while expanding TJ's outreach on the greater region. This platform, proposed by the administration, was built with students in mind and TJ Consulting was a big part of realizing the ideas of students within JCIRN.
